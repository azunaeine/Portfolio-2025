About Me

Hi! I'm Azuna Ekundayo, based in Edmonton, Alberta. I graduated from Sheridan College's Interaction Design program in 2025, where I built a solid foundation in design, user research, and coding. Over time, I've had the chance to intern at a few places, which really helped shape the way I think creatively and approach digital problem-solving.

Outside of that, I run my own creative agency, redbeamdigital.com. I offer design, development, and digital marketing services—and yes, I designed and coded my own portfolio from scratch using Figma and front-end tools. I love blending storytelling with clean, functional design.

Right now, I'm focused on growing as a well-rounded designer by improving my skills in user-centered design, front-end coding, and digital marketing. I'm also working on building Red Beam Digital into a full-service agency that helps brands connect with their audiences in meaningful, engaging ways.

If you ever want to chat, feel free to reach out by email or phone—I'm always open to connecting!
