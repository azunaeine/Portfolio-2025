About Me

Hi, I’m Azuna Ekundayo from Edmonton, Alberta. I graduated from Sheridan College’s Interaction Design program in 2025, where I developed a strong foundation in design, user research, and coding. Over the years, I’ve gained valuable hands-on experience through multiple internships, which helped shape my creative thinking and approach to digital problem-solving. In my free time, I run my own creative agency, redbeamdigital.com, where I offer services in design, development, and digital marketing. I designed and built my personal portfolio from scratch using Figma and code, combining visual storytelling with technical execution. Looking ahead, I’m focused on growing as a professional designer and building Red Beam Digital into a full-service agency that helps brands connect with their audiences through thoughtful, user-centered design. When I’m not designing, you can usually find me watching a new TV series, diving into a good book, or experimenting with new coding techniques. Feel free to reach out by email or phone—I’m always open to connecting.
